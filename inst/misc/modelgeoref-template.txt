# Model Geo-Reference Information

Vertical coordinate information is referenced to the North American Vertical Datum of 1988
([NAVD88](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_American_Vertical_Datum_of_1988)).
Horizontal coordinate information is referenced to the North American Datum of 1983 and projected into the Idaho Transverse Mercator projection
([IDTM83](https://www.idwr.idaho.gov/GIS/IDTM/)).

Corners of the rectangle outlining the model domain are described below.
Spatial points are specified within two coordinate reference system's ([CRS](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spatial_reference_system));
a CRS is defined using a string of [PROJ.4](https://trac.osgeo.org/proj/wiki/GenParms) parameters.
